How to Use:

Select Chord Type: Choose between Major and Minor triad arpeggios

Select a CAGED Shape: Choose from C, A, G, E, or D chord shapes

Adjust Root Fret: Move the slider or select a key from the dropdown to transpose the pattern

Display Mode: Toggle between "Pattern Only" (dots) and "Show All Notes" (note names)

In Pattern Mode: Red dots (R) = Root notes, Blue dots (●) = Other chord tones

In Notes Mode: All note names are shown, with pattern notes highlighted in color

Practice playing these patterns to master both major and minor triad arpeggios across the entire fretboard!
